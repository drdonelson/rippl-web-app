Hi [First Name],

Let's get you live. I need five things — most offices have these ready in about 10 minutes. Reply to this email with them and I'll have the integration running within 24-48 hours.

  • 1
    Open Dental server URL
    The web address your eConnector is running on — usually something like http://192.168.x.x:8080 or a public URL if you're cloud-hosted. Your IT team or Open Dental rep will know this.
  • 2
    Open Dental Customer Key
    Found in Open Dental under Setup → eServices → eConnector. It's called the "API Customer Key" — a long string of letters and numbers. (Developer Key is different — don't need that one.)
  • 3
    Practice logo
    PNG or SVG preferred, any size. Used on your patient-facing referral page. If you don't have a digital file handy, a JPEG works fine too.
  • 4
    Gift card reward amount
    How much do you want to reward patients who successfully refer someone? Standard is $35. Some practices go $50 for higher engagement. You can change this anytime.
  • 5
    Patient-facing practice name
    What patients see in their texts and emails — usually just your practice name. Let me know if you want something slightly different (e.g., "Hallmark Family Dental" vs "Hallmark Dental").

Just hit reply and paste those in. If any of them take a minute to track down, no worries — send what you have and we'll fill in the gaps on a quick call.
