Co-Marketing & Product Rights

Co-marketing: CarLock agrees that Rippl may identify CarLock as an automotive Founding Partner in marketing materials, the Rippl website, and investor/partner presentations. CarLock may approve or request modification of any specific use within 5 business days of notice; silence constitutes approval.

Case study: Upon CarLock's approval (not to be unreasonably withheld), Rippl may publish aggregated referral performance data as a case study. No individual customer data will be included.

Product steering: CarLock will receive one quarterly product steering call with Rippl leadership to influence the automotive feature roadmap. Roadmap decisions remain at Rippl's discretion.

Reference: CarLock agrees to be available as a reference for potential Rippl automotive clients in non-competing markets, up to 2 reference calls per year.

Data & Compliance

Data ownership: All customer data remains the property of CarLock. Rippl processes it solely to operate the referral platform and does not sell, share, or use it for any other purpose.

TCPA compliance: CarLock represents that it has an existing business relationship with all customers imported at launch, sufficient for CAN-SPAM-compliant email outreach. SMS messages are sent only after explicit opt-in captured via the Rippl referral platform. CarLock is responsible for ensuring its customer list meets applicable legal requirements at import time.

DriveCentric credentials: SFTP credentials are stored encrypted in Rippl's production environment (Render/Supabase) and used solely for deal data export processing. Credentials are never logged or shared.
