How It Works

Four steps. Zero staff involvement.

Step 01
New patient visits the practice
Within 2 hours, Rippl sends an automated welcome text — including a simple referral link and onboarding to the rewards program.
SMS · Automatic
Step 02
Practice management software logs the visit
Rippl syncs with Open Dental (dental) or Vagaro (salon) every 5 minutes, watching for procedure codes that signal a referred patient — no manual data entry.
Open Dental · Vagaro
Step 03
Referrer is automatically matched and rewarded
Rippl identifies the patient who made the referral, updates their reward tier, and sends a Tango gift card by email and SMS. No staff steps required.
Tango Card · Instant
Step 04
Practice sees everything in the dashboard
Every referral, reward, and patient tier is logged. Unmatched cases surface as a simple Tasks queue with a one-click dropdown fix — rare but handled.
Real-time Dashboard
