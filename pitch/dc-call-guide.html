The Problem — referrals are invisible
2–5 min
  • Independent practices run almost entirely on word-of-mouth — but they have no system to see it, measure it, or reward it
  • Front desk asks "how'd you hear about us?" and forgets by 3pm — that's the entire referral tracking system for most practices
  • The patient who referred a friend hears nothing back — no thank you, no reward, no reason to do it again
  • Referrals are the highest-trust, lowest-cost acquisition channel in dentistry. Most of it is completely invisible.
Your members' best patients are already sending them referrals. They just can't see it, and they're not rewarding it. Rippl fixes that automatically.
2
How Rippl Works — zero staff steps
5–10 min
  • Connects to Open Dental (or Vagaro) — syncs every 5 minutes, watches for new patient procedure codes
  • New patient visits → automated welcome text goes out within 2 hours with a referral link
  • Referral detected → Rippl matches the new patient to who referred them, automatically
  • Reward sent → Tango gift card by email + SMS to the referrer. No staff involved at any step.

Who else is doing this?
No direct competitor at this price point for independent dental. Birdeye and NexHealth do reputation management — not referral automation. This is a gap in the market.
What about HIPAA?
Rippl doesn't store PHI. We pull procedure codes and patient names for matching only — same data used by Weave or Podium. No clinical records touched.
What PMS systems?
Open Dental today (most DC members likely use this or Dentrix). Vagaro for non-dental. Dentrix integration is next on the roadmap.
Can I see the product?
Yes — pull up dc-deck.html and walk through slides 3–5. Or offer to do a separate 30-min demo with their screen on the live dashboard.
