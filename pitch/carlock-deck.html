Two-layer attribution.
Zero staff steps.
Two independent ways to capture a referral — either one is enough. Together, nothing gets lost.
Layer 1 — Referral Link + Inquiry Form
Capture it before she buys
  • Billy buys → Rippl sends his personal link via SMS + email
  • Sue clicks it and fills out an inquiry form → her name and Billy's code stored server-side before she touches any paid channel
  • Works even if she returns 10 times from ads before signing
Layer 2 — DriveCentric Survey at Point of Sale
Capture it when the deal closes
  • Salesperson asks "Who referred you?" — Sue says Billy's name
  • That answer goes into the DriveCentric deal survey
  • Rippl reads the SFTP feed, matches the name to Billy, fires the reward — automatically
Why two layers matters
Either one is enough
  • Layer 1 catches it early — even if the survey is missed at close
  • Layer 2 catches it at close — even if Sue never clicked the link
  • No referral lost — both paths lead to the same match
What changes immediately
Referrals become visible
  • Every word-of-mouth deal tracked and attributed
  • Billy gets rewarded → he refers again → flywheel starts
  • Your CRM stops giving Google credit for your customers' work
