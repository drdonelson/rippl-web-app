Root Cause

Three reasons it never gets fixed.

01
Manual entry fails
Salespeople have 50+ things to enter into a CRM. "Who referred you?" gets skipped. Every. Time. You can't build a referral program on manual data.
02
Digital ads claim the close
Last-touch attribution gives the credit to whatever ad Sue clicked before buying — even if Billy's word-of-mouth is what started the journey 3 weeks ago.
03
No reward = no tracking
Billy has no incentive to tell you he referred Sue. Without a reward mechanism, the referral stays invisible to both Billy and your marketing team.
⚠️ Industry estimate: 20–30% of all automotive buyers come from referrals — but most dealers have no way to track, reward, or scale it.
