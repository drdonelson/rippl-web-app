Know Your Audience — CMO Mindset
Read before the call
A CMO thinks in cost per acquisition, channel ROI, and attribution. They're also protective of their marketing budget and skeptical of new vendors. Your edges: (1) referrals close at 50–70% vs. 5–10% for paid ads, and (2) the attribution problem on referrals is real, unsolved, and costs them money every month. The DriveCentric quote in the deck is your sharpest weapon — it came from their own ecosystem. Use it early.

The Attribution Problem — Billy and Sue
3–8 min
  • Billy tells Sue about CarLock — Sue goes home, Googles CarLock, clicks a retargeting ad, fills out a form. Your CRM logs: Source = Google Ads. Billy gets nothing.
  • This happens on every referral — paid ads get credit for word-of-mouth your customer earned
  • You're paying Google for referrals your own customers generated
  • And because Billy never hears back, he has no reason to do it again — the flywheel never starts
A DriveCentric executive told us directly: "Billy refers Sue to CarLock, she goes online and does most of the process from an online ad, and Billy never gets the attribution. It appears as an organic lead from the internet." That's the exact problem we solve — and it came from inside your own CRM ecosystem.

Discovery Questions — listen, don't pitch
13–18 min

These answers let you price this correctly. Take notes. Don't fill the silence — let them talk.

Deal Mix
"Across your 7 stores, what's the rough split between mainstream brands and your luxury stores — Bentley and others?"
You need to know if Bentley is 1 store or 3. A Bentley referral is a completely different economic event than a Honda referral.
Referral Volume Guess
"If you had to guess, how many deals a month do you think come in through word-of-mouth but get attributed to something else?"
Anchors the value of the problem. Even a conservative answer (5–10/mo) tells you this is worth serious money to them.
Reward Philosophy
"What kind of reward do you think would actually motivate your customers to refer? Does that change for someone who just bought a Bentley vs. a Cadillac?"
Tells you their reward budget per segment. A Bentley buyer probably expects $500–$1,000 — which completely changes the platform economics.
DriveCentric Coverage
"Are all 7 stores on DriveCentric, or is the Bentley store on a different CRM?"
Bentley franchises sometimes use a manufacturer-mandated CRM. If it's not DriveCentric, the integration path is different.
Here's our pricing — it's $299 a month plus $50 per deal.
I'm intentionally not throwing a number at you today. Once I understand your deal mix and what reward values make sense for your different brands, I can put together pricing that actually reflects the value on both sides — a Bentley referral and a Civic referral shouldn't cost the same.

If They Ask…
Have ready
What does this cost?
"I'm not going to quote you a number today — not because I'm hiding something, but because your deal mix with 7 stores including Bentley means a flat rate would either shortchange you or shortchange me. Two weeks after today I'll have something that actually fits."
Why free during beta?
"Because the DriveCentric integration doesn't exist yet — I'm building it. You're doing me a favor by being first. It would be wrong to charge you while we're still proving it works."
What if it doesn't work?
"You walk away having spent nothing. The beta is genuinely free. If the integration doesn't produce referral attribution data that impresses you, there's no obligation to go to Stage 2."
What's the DriveCentric email?
"It's a simple consent to activate the SFTP data feed — something like 'We authorize DriveCentric to share deal data with Rippl (BD America LLC) for referral tracking purposes.' I can draft the exact language for you."
How long does setup take?
"Two to three weeks from DriveCentric consent to live: one week to coordinate the SFTP feed, one week to configure and test, a few days of soft-launch. You'll see the first referrals tracked within a month of the consent email."
Can I see the product?
Pull up carlock-deck.html and walk slides 3–5. Or offer a 30-min demo of the live Hallmark Dental dashboard — same admin experience they'd get, with real referral data running.
