The Solution

Referrals: automatic, measurable,
and self-compounding.

Step 01
📲
Patient enrolled automatically
2 hours after first appointment, every new patient gets a personal referral link by text. No front desk involvement. Ever.
Step 02
💬
Patient shares their link
By text, social media, word-of-mouth. The link locks in attribution before the referred friend ever searches online.
Step 03 — the key
🔍
Open Dental detects the referral
Rippl polls Open Dental every 5 minutes for completed referral procedure codes. Detection is automatic — no staff step required.
Step 04
🎁
Reward fires instantly
Gift card link sent by text and email within minutes. Tier system: more referrals = higher reward. Best referrers self-identify and accelerate.
💡 One Rippl account covers all locations. Every referral, every location, every reward — one dashboard. The bigger the group, the more the referral network compounds across the patient base.
